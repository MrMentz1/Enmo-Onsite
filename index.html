Services

What Enmo Onsite offers

All programs are delivered by licensed physical therapists with hands-on industrial experience. We flex to your shift schedule, facility layout, and workforce size.

01

Injury prevention & early intervention

Our PTs conduct musculoskeletal screenings, identify at-risk employees, and deliver brief interventions before pain becomes an injury. This is the highest-leverage program for reducing recordables and controlling claim frequency.

Most popular
02

OSHA first aid management

On-site licensed PT triage means injuries that might otherwise become recordable OSHA incidents are handled at the first-aid level — legally and properly. We document everything and coordinate with your safety team.

Cost-avoidance focused
03

Ergonomic job analysis

We assess workstations, lifting tasks, and repetitive motions to identify risk factors, then recommend practical solutions that fit your production constraints — not theoretical changes your floor can't implement.

Reduces repetitive strain claims
04

Stretch-and-flex / movement programs

Structured warm-up and mobility programs delivered to teams at the start of shifts. Research-backed, job-specific, and designed to build a culture of movement awareness in your workforce.

Team-based
05

Workforce education & toolbox talks

Short, practical talks on body mechanics, lifting technique, hydration, and fatigue management — delivered in the language of your workforce and tied to your real job tasks.

About

Built by a PT who's been on the floor

Enmo Onsite was founded by a licensed physical therapist with direct experience providing PT services inside industrial facilities — not just consulting from the outside.

Most workplace health programs are designed by people who've never watched a line worker pull 10-hour shifts. Enmo Onsite is different. Our approach is grounded in firsthand experience at real manufacturing and distribution facilities in the Bay Area.

We understand production pressure, shift schedules, safety culture, and the skepticism workers have toward corporate wellness programs. We show up ready to earn trust — and we do it by actually helping.
